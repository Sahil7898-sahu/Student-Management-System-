# 📋 Getting Your Google Sheet URL - Quick Steps

## Option 1: Using the Button (Recommended)

1. **Open your Google Sheet** (the one created by the script)
2. **Look for the menu**: "Student Management System" 
3. **Click "Get Web App URL"**
4. **Copy the URL** from the popup
5. **Paste it here** 👇

## Option 2: Manual Method

1. **Go to Google Apps Script**: https://script.google.com
2. **Open your project**
3. **Click "Deploy" → "Manage deployments"**
4. **Copy the Web app URL**

## Option 3: From Script Editor

1. **In your Google Apps Script project**
2. **Run the function**: `getWebAppUrl`
3. **Copy the URL from the result**

## 🎯 What You're Looking For

Your URL should look like:
```
https://script.google.com/macros/s/ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ123456789/exec
```

## 📝 Paste Your URL Below

Once you have your URL, you can:
1. Open your `index-new.html`
2. When the setup modal appears, paste the URL
3. Click "Connect to Google Sheets"

## 🔍 If You Need Help

- Make sure you've deployed the web app
- Check that permissions are authorized
- Ensure the script is saved
- Try refreshing the Google Sheet page

The button method is the easiest - just click and copy!
